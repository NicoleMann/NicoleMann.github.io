This section is where I've put the stories I've written. Most of them are romance-ish but I tried to branch ou a little. The horror story Father's Love is my particular favourite. This is the one I show people when they ask to see a sample of my writing.

Father's Love - Maxwell Cifer's adoptive father has escaped from prison and returned to wreak havoc on Maxwell's friends.

Fixing Adam - Amy can't deal with her twin brother's self destructive habits anymore.

Shift - Ryuuchi is an outcast among outcasts but as long as Kamitani is there with him, he might be okay.

Blood and Chocolate - Suguro has unknowningly stumbled upon a nest of demons when all he wanted was a date with a pair of bright blue eyes.
