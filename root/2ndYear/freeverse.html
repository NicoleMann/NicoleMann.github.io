Twins

The One and the Other are twins

Identical, interchangeable

Only one legitimate difference

One hurts, Other heals.

 

Because One has the evil of One and Other

And Other has the good of Other and One

 

Never approach One by himself

You would never escape unharmed

Such an encounter would turn disastrous

For those prone to nightmares.

 

Because One has the evil of One and Other

And Other has the good of Other and One

 

Other always distracts One

Takes the bullying on himself

Never snaps, never fights back

Never even gets angry

 

Because One has the evil of One and Other

And Other has the good of Other and One

 

Two sides of the exact same coin

Mirror image and yet reversed

One and Other, Other and One

Only two, 'cause it's that or none.

 

The Faces He Shows

Tall and thick

Eyes dark and brooding

Lips firmly frowning

A brute?

 

Bloody knuckles

A dark back alley

Alcohol on his breath

A drunk?

Mental contradictions

Backwards words

Actions upside down

A head case?

 

Does what he has to

Looks out for his own

Accepts her as is

A heart of gold.
